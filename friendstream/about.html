    Welcome to Pintweet


    Pintweet let's you to view your twitter tweets in Pinterest wall style. You can view the most tweets that you are interested in without following the tweet's owner.


    Features

    • View tweets in Pinterest wall style, more easy to view and navigete.
    • Can view the most tweets that you are interested in without following the tweet's owner.
    • Can use this site to directly retweet and reply.
    • Can use this site to write tweets.
    • Use OAuth2 authentication for twitter account access (secure and fast).
    • It is free with no ads!

    Recent updates

    • Added "about" page for the site May 21, 2012
    • Support Pinterest style page navigation May 17, 2012
    • Use twitter button to write tweets directly from the site May 15, 2012
    • Support retweet and reply in tweets May 14, 2012
